What is Shopify Dropshipping?

Shopify dropshipping is a revolutionary business model that allows you to sell products online without holding inventory. When a customer places an order, you simply forward it to your supplier, who ships directly to the customer.

This means you can start an e-commerce business with minimal upfront investment, no warehouse costs, and the freedom to work from anywhere in the world. It's the perfect entry point for aspiring entrepreneurs.
